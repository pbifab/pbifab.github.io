Target Markets

Serving Industries That Run on Data

PBIFab works with mid-market to enterprise organizations across high-stakes, data-intensive sectors.

Our solutions are purpose-built for industries where data accuracy, speed, and clarity directly impact bottom lines, compliance posture, and operational efficiency. We understand the nuances of each sector we serve.

🏦
FINANCE & FINTECH

Finance & Fintech

Financial services organizations need analytics that are fast, accurate, and audit-ready. PBIFab delivers real-time dashboards built for regulatory compliance, risk monitoring, and executive reporting.

  • Real-time trading and portfolio performance dashboards
  • Regulatory compliance reporting (Basel III, IFRS, etc.)
  • Fraud detection visualizations and alerts
  • Customer profitability and segmentation analysis
  • Month-end and quarter-end financial close automation
🛒
RETAIL & E-COMMERCE

Retail & E-commerce

Retail success depends on visibility — from supplier to shelf to sale. We build analytics ecosystems that connect supply chain, inventory, sales, and customer behavior in one unified view.

  • Supply chain visibility and vendor performance tracking
  • Customer behavior, funnel, and retention analytics
  • Inventory optimization and demand forecasting
  • Store vs. online channel performance comparison
  • Marketing spend ROI and attribution dashboards
🏭
MANUFACTURING

Manufacturing

Modern manufacturing generates enormous volumes of IoT and operational data. PBIFab helps you visualize machine health, production efficiency, and quality metrics in real time.

  • IoT sensor data visualization and anomaly detection
  • OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) dashboards
  • Production line throughput and downtime tracking
  • Quality control and defect rate reporting
  • Supply chain and procurement analytics
🏥
HEALTHCARE

Healthcare

Healthcare analytics must balance clinical accuracy with data privacy. We build HIPAA-aware analytics solutions that give clinicians and administrators the insights they need to improve outcomes.

  • Patient outcome and readmission rate analysis
  • Hospital resource utilization and bed capacity tracking
  • Clinical trial data visualization
  • Revenue cycle management and billing analytics
  • Staff scheduling and workforce optimization
