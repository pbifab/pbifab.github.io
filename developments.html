Company Updates

Milestones & Announcements

🔷

Microsoft Fabric Adoption — Full Service Launch

PBIFab has pivoted to become a key enabler for organizations adopting the new Microsoft Fabric SaaS platform. We now offer specialized 'Fabric Readiness Assessments' — a structured evaluation of your current data estate's readiness for Fabric migration, covering architecture, governance, and cost modeling. This service has become our fastest-growing offering.

🤖

Microsoft Copilot Integration Practice

With the rollout of Copilot capabilities in Power BI and Fabric, PBIFab has developed a dedicated practice around AI-powered report generation, natural language Q&A, and automated insight generation. Our team now offers Copilot enablement workshops for enterprise clients.

🌐

Community Engagement — Power BI User Groups

PBIFab team members are now active participants in multiple Power BI User Groups globally. We contribute through knowledge-sharing sessions, technical blogs, and public Q&A forums — solidifying our position as thought leaders in the Microsoft analytics ecosystem.

🏅

Team Certification Milestone

100% of PBIFab's development team now holds active Microsoft certifications including PL-300 and DP-600. This milestone reinforces our commitment to delivering verified, cutting-edge expertise to every client engagement.

🚀

Direct Lake Mode Expertise

PBIFab has developed deep expertise in Direct Lake mode — one of the most impactful features in Microsoft Fabric. Our team has successfully implemented Direct Lake-powered semantic models for clients, delivering query performance improvements of 10x compared to traditional import models.

📋

Migration Accelerators Released

We've developed proprietary migration accelerators for Tableau-to-Power BI and Qlik-to-Power BI transitions, significantly reducing time-to-value for clients making the switch. These tools automate up to 60% of the visual and data model migration process.
